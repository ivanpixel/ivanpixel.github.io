Box Jellyfish

Hello, and welcome to Aussie Sealines' Box Jellyfish Safety Manual! The box jellyfish is a dangerous marine creature that you may come across during your travels with us. If you do not know what a box jellyfish is, or if you do not fully understand the proper safety precautions that must be taken when a box jellyfish is present, please read about them on our site. If you have any questions that have not been addressed on our site, you can contact us using the information in our contact page. Thank you for your support.
